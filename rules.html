OFFICIAL LEAGUE RULES (subject to change- lets talk it through if something seems off)
- We each have 2 teams split across 4 divisions
- Each team will have 12 players
- Draft budget is $480 each ($20/player)
- There will be no trades in between the teams we manage
- Each division has 2 of our teams with no duplicate matchups (like Lew and Paul are only in 1 division together and Lew's other team's division is with me)
- There are no injuries
- We can make 1 add/drop per week
- I will simulate a weeks worth of games and update our site every Sunday (we can adjust this cadence if we want to do it more frequently- its a pretty easy workflow for me)
- There is a section for rivalries where the site tracks our head to head records against each other
- The site keeps track of awards like MVP and DPOY, pretty cool :)
